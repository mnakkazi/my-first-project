Interests

My interests include:

  • I enjoy spending my free time reading novels. Adventures and Thrillers are my favorite.

  • I enjoy watching great movies too, not excluding TV shows of course. Currently can't get enough of 'Stranger Things'. That show is a living work of art and I know to appreciate good writing when I see it.
